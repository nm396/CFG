About Me

Welcome

My name is Naomi and I love food. I love food a lot! like... a lot! Honestly, I think that's what made going vegan so much easier. I love to try new recipes and experiment in the kitchen, so I've considered the whole thing a culinary adventure.

I was lucky to have someone show me the ropes and share their veganly wisdom with me. This is my chance to share those tips with you so you too can find it easier to succeed in vegan awesomeness.

This is a collection of recipe ideas, some of my own, some from friends, and some from my favourite food bloggers. There's also a section for tips (because sometimes we just need to know how to settle that craving) and useful websites. So whether you are new to the vegan world or a long-term vegan looking just some inspiration enjoy these recipes! Most importantly share delicious meals with your friends.

Oh and don't be afraid to get creative in the kitchen.
