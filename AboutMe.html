About Me

Hello, I'm Naomi.
I love food! I think that's why going vegan hasn't been difficult for me. I like to try new and interesting recipes so the whole thing has been an exciting adventure for me. This isn't a "preachy" vegan website, I don't think that approach gets you very far. Before I went vegan I had admired those that could do it but thought it was just too hard for me, I mean it's so restrictive, right? and I have a massive sweet tooth so how could I give up cookies and chocolate?...
But then I met my current partner, who had been vegan already for 4 years, and when I saw what he could eat I realised there was so much that I thought. That's why I believe sharing food and experiences with eachother is the most valuable tool we have.
This website is for those who are curious about the vegan lifestyle and want some recipe ideas, it's also for the long-term vegans who have found themselves in a bit of a food rut. So please enjoy these recipes and share some delicious meals with your friends!
