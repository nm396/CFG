About Me

Hello, welcome!

First things first I love food! I think that's why going vegan hasn't really been difficult for me. I love to try new recipes, and experiment in the kitchen, so the whole thing has been an exciting culinary adventure for me.

This isn't a "preachy" vegan website, I don't think that approach gets you very far. It never gave me encouragement before I went vegan. I had admired those that could do it but thought it was just too hard for me, I mean "it's so restrictive, right? and besides I have a massive sweet tooth so how could I give up cookies and chocolate?...

But then I met my current partner, who had been vegan for 4 years already, and when I saw what he could eat I realised there was so much more available to me. There were more replacements than I realised, I just hadn't paid attention before. That's why I believe sharing food and experiences with eachother is the most valuable tool we have.

This website is for those who are curious about the vegan lifestyle and who want some recipe ideas, there's also advice on food replacements and information on where you can get your protein; it's also for the long-term vegans who have found themselves in a bit of a food rut.

So please enjoy these recipes and share some delicious meals with your friends! Oh and get creative in the kitchen.
