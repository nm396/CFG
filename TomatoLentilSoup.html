Spicy Tomato and Lentil Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • Small piece of Ginger
  • (optional) 1/2 Chilli
  • 500g Tomatoes (plum tomatoes work well)
  • 1 Cup (175g) Red Lentils
  • 1 Vegetable Stock Cube
  • 1 heaped tsp Cumin
  • 1/2 tsp Cayenne Pepper

Method

Finely chop the onion, garlic, ginger and chilli (if desired). Roughly chop the tomatoes and wash the lentils

Fry the onion and garlic on a low heat for 5-10mins

Add the ginger, tomatoes, lentils, cumin and cayenne pepper (and chilli) and stir well

Add 1.2 litres of boiled water and the stock cube. Turn heat up to bring to the boil. Once boiling reduce heat and let simmer for 30mins.

Blend together and add salt and pepper to taste.
